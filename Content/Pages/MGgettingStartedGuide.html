Monogame Getting Started Guide

The Getting Started Guide was written in about 3 weeks during August 2016.
It's aim is to be a simple explanation of how to use Monogame to do basic tasks.
Such things as drawing images and fonts to the screen are explained.
Other concepts like input, classes, and full screen programs are discussed as well.

This guide's content was based on my experiences developing games for the xbox360 + Pc, using XNA.
Moving from XNA to Monogame seemed like it would be easy, but there were subtle issues.
In a way, this guide is a record of how I solved certain problems, and as such is not all inclusive.
The Guide's public repo is located here: https://github.com/MrGrak/Monogame-Getting-Started
