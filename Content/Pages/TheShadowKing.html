The Shadow King

A top-down 2D zelda-game, where you play a hero who defeats bosses in dungeons and coliseums.
The Game's public repo is located here: https://github.com/MrGrak/TSK

This project is on going and still in development, which started on March 12, 2017.
This project's current goal is to be a new and well done Zelda game.
It's secondary goal is to be an example of pure mutable game design, at scale.




The Shadow King initially started as just an attempt to make my own Zelda game.
Once that was achieved, I started experimenting with what was possible.
I just wanted to make Zelda better and funner, but doing so required alot of time.
I've created a page that shows The Development Timeline.
